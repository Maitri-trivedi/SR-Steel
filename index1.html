WELCOME TO S.R.STEELS

S.R. Steels, founded in 1980, is a leading manufacturer of Spares, Industrial Valves & Accessories. Designed keeping in mind the strict quality norms, our valves are in compliance with national and international quality standards.We promise you impeccable quality in our valves assists us in earning accolades from huge clientele based in India. Our focus is to develop the customer base nationally & internationally and ensure best services at all times. We believe in ‘Make In India’ and we source our raw materials from vendors and third party suppliers across the country. We have manufacturing unit in Vasai, Mumbai that is manufacturing quality products for over three decades now.

A vision to bring quality engineering products and machine components became reality in 1982 when two technocrats decided to bring their heads and brains together and started S R Steels. We started as pattern making process unit and gradually we gave it a shape by widening our product range that is backed by superior technical expertise.
