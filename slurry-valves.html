SLURRY VALVES

WHY ANGLE SLURRY VALVES ??

Angle Slurry Valves are specifically designed for application in industries where erosion and scaling are frequent. They have a special disc turning device designed to help grind the seats clean. They come in Angle, Bayonet and Y Globe design

ADVANTAGE OF ANGLE SLURRY VALVE

The fundamental difference between the two is that the fluid flow through the angle valve makes a 90° turn. These valves offer less resistance to flow than a globe valve with an elbow which it would replace. An angle valve reduces the number joints in a line, in addition to saving installation time.

WHAT IS DRIVING DEMAND FOR SLURRY VALVES

Slurry Valves used to regulate the flow of slurries in process industries. Increasing concern for industry waste treatment is anticipated to augment growth for this market in the future. The recent increase in demand for this valves by pharmaceutical companies due to the outbreak of coronavirus has further boosted the market growth.
